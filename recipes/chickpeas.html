Roasted Chickpeas

Really?

Yes, really. Roasted Chickpeas are a delicious high-fiber snack. They're oven-roasted and seasoned to taste with cayenne and garlic salt.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees
  2. Blot chickpeas with a paper towel to dry them
  3. Toss chickpeas and olive oil in bowl. Season to taste with garlic salt, cayenne, and salt and toss again.
  4. Spread chickpeas on a rimmed baking sheet.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven until browned and crunchy, 30 to 40 minutes; watch carefully to avoid burning them.
